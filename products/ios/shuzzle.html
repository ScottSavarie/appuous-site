Shuzzle

An engaging and deceptively simple puzzle game for the iPhone.

Shuzzle is an engaging and deceptively simple puzzle game that’s fun, interactive, and addictive. The purpose of the game is to match shapes to colors while under the stress of a timer. The timer speeds up the longer you play making it more difficult every second. The objective in Shuzzle is to match the center shape with one of the six surrounding shapes. One point is earned for just a shape match and ten points for both shape and color!

Dual Controls

One mode allows for users to simply tap one of the six surrounding shapes with their finger. This mode is called simply called touch. In tilt mode, simply tile your device in the direction of the circle you’d like the center piece to move to. It's as simple as that! Two great game modes and five deceptively simple levels.

Power Ups

  • Doubler

    Earn 2 points for shape matches and 20 points for color matches.

  • Radiation

    Replace all of the surrounding shapes with new ones.

  • Slowdown

    Slowdown the timer.

  • Freeze

    Freezes the clock in blitz mode.

  • Extra Life / Extra Time

    Gain an extra life or 10 extra seconds in blitz mode.

  • Uniformity

    Replace all active game pieces with blue pieces to make all possible color matches.

Features

  • Game Center

  • Advanced Piece

  • Play Your Own Tunes

  • Colorblind Play

    Enable colorblind game pieces.

  • Golden Shape
