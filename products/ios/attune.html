Attune

Recapture your library. Simplify your music.

Attune is part genius, part simplifier. Attune scans your library playing 10 second previews of songs until you find one you like. Simply tap the song you want to listen to and Attune will craft a playlist built around that song. With Attune, you no longer have to know what you want to listen to — you’ll know when you hear it.

Smart Playlist Creation

Using an advanced rating system and Last.fm integration Attune matches your library to what you’re in the mood for. We’ve always been big fans of music, but we were always trying to find that one song that had gotten lost in our library. The way you listen to music has changed. Gone are the days where you have to know what you want to listen to. Attune does the work for you.

Gesture Based

Attune is completely gesture based making it easy to use. Tap to play/pause; two finger swipe up/down to control volume; pinch to start a new session;
